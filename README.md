# risk-radar

> 🇰🇷 [한국어 README](./README.ko.md)

**Proactive risk prediction and monitoring engine. Pre-identify risks across projects, businesses, investments, and organizations. Structures risks with probability × impact matrices and supports periodic scanning.**

## Goal
Move from reactive risk management to proactive risk intelligence. This engine helps you systematically identify emerging risks across your portfolio, quantify their potential impact, and establish continuous monitoring to catch warning signals before they become crises.

## Key Features
- **Multi-Domain Risk Taxonomy**: Project, business, investment, and organizational risk frameworks
- **Pre-Identification Protocol**: Structured discovery of hidden risks using domain-specific patterns
- **Probability × Impact Matrices**: Quantitative risk scoring and prioritization by severity
- **Warning Signal System**: Define leading indicators and trigger thresholds for each risk
- **Periodic Scan Protocol**: Automated risk assessment cycles with trend analysis and escalation rules
